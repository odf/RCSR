bounds

This section takes numerical input, which can be a single number, a range which is specified by two numbers separated by a dash, or a less than or greater than symbol followed by a number.

Examples:

InputCondition
2 the value (e.g. kinds of vertex) is exactly 2
1-2 the value is between 1 and 2 (inclusive)
1.0-1.2 the value (e.g. density) is between 1.0 and 1.2 (inclusive)
<5 the value is strictly smaller than 5
>0.8 the value is strictly larger than 0.8
